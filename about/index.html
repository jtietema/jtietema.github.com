About Me

Hi! I am a Freelance Android developer. I live in Utrecht, The Netherlands.

Besides developing Android apps I love the Open Source software and the web. I am a co-organizer of HackersNL and I love to talk about technology.

When not coding I like to spend time outdoors kayaking, climbing, snowboarding or surfing. I really try to make up for my deskjob.

Hire me

Like I said I work as a freelancer / consultant. If you need some extra guns on an Android project, don’t hesitate to contact me.

Some projects I have worked on:

  • Salomon Powfinder - The kind people of Frismedia hired me to help them develop the Android version of the Powfinder.
  • Nieuwe Wetten - SPRIGS hired me to finish a project that was already underway.
  • Voetbal International - During my employment at The Saints I worked on the EuroCup 2012 version and the 2012-2013 Season version of the app.
